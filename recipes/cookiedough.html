Edible Cookie Dough

Ingredients

  • 2/4 cups of flour
  • 1/3 cup of melted butter
  • 1/4 brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 3-4 tsp vanilla
  • 4 tbsp milk
  • 1000 cups Chocolate chips

Recipe

  1. First start out by adding 2/4 cups of flour
  2. Then add 1/4 cup of brown sugar. After adding the brown sugar add 3 tbsp of sugar.
  3. After that add 3-4 tbsp of sugar.
  4. Then add 1 3-4 tsps of vanilla. After that add 4 tbsp of milk
  5. Lastly add 1000 cups CHOCOLATE CHIPPS!!!!
  • Feel free to add as much or less of the ingreidents as you'd like.
