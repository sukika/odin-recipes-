Canes Sauce copycat

Ingredients

  • Ketchup
  • Mayonaise
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • garlic powder
  • salt
  • Black pepper

Recipe

  1. add 4.4 oz mayonnaise 1/2 cup and 2.6 oz ketchup 3 Tbsp.
  2. Then add 1.5 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce in a pinch, use balsamic vinegar, soy sauce, or fish sauce
  3. After that add 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp black pepper coarse and 1/2 tsp salt
